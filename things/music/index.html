Listening

I listen to, what I personally see as, A LOT of music. I primarily enjoy music from ~1990 onwards with electronic influences. I find most of my music by browsing record stores and places like Discogs and Bandcamp.

I strongly dislike streaming services. I primarily listen to music on my iPod classic, by playing my CDs, or using Feishin.

If you want to know what I’m listening to right now, you can check my Last.fm. I’ve also written about my favorite music of 2024.

I’m always looking for new tunes, so if you have any recommendations please get in touch!

Making

I run a netlabel called Mlem Records, we release music online and prioritize creative value and accessibility. Most music is therefore released under CC BY-NC, to allow for free redistribution and adaptation, while artist retain commercial rights.

I have released musical projects mostly under the aliases DJ ZITwerK, love sella and am a part of the band The Brainworms and the rap group Bille.
