Description

Andie's a drum machine that's great at making weird, glitchy and unexpected beats. That you can then remix with punch-in effects.

Use Andie's quirky, random nature to find sounds and rhythms you like. Or, don't settle. Simply let it go wild!

Andie is compatible with existing musical hardware, like Pocket Operators, just by using a regular aux cable.

Comes in three flavors: LightüîÜ, darküåô and strawberryüçì.

Whatever noise you're making, we'll make it work. Andie's modular, so you can customize it to your needs to get the sounds and rhythms you're looking for.

Andie is currently crowdfunding on Kickstarter. Once funded and fully developed, Andie will be available on iOS and Android devices.
