About

Andie is an 8 channel semi-modular drum machine made for performance, exploration and play.

Andie's available on iOS and Android devices.

Made in the Unity engine. Using in-house audio synthesis engine "Bloop".

 

Interface

Home screen

The home screen consists of three major parts: The mixing section, the performance section and the bottom bar.

On this screen the top bar shows only the play button and the DSP load.

The mixing section shows busses corresponding to each of Andie's channels. Each bus contains a show button, solo button, mute button and volume picker.

The performance section shows a large play button when Andie is not playing. When playing, the punch-in effects are revealed, which can be held to remix the current composition. These punch-in effects are, in order: Fill, Random, Crush, Bit, Reverse, Echo, Wobble and Scratch.

TIP: You can touch Andie's face whilst not playing to make them scream!

Below the punch-in effect buttons, two master effects are shown. These modify Andie's output. The first master effect is a Waveshaper, the second master effect is a Crush.

The bottom bar contains the metronome toggle, BPM picker and settings button.

 

Channel screen

The channel screen consists of two major parts: The voice section and the sequencer section.

On this screen the top bar shows the back button (to return to the home screen), the channel info button, the play button and the DSP load. You can tap the center of the top bar to mute/unmute the channel. Muted channels will show as greyed-out. Image to be added.

The voice section is dedicated to controlling to the channel's sound. The first two parameters, regardless of the voice, are volume and panning.

The sequencer section is dedicated to controlling a channel's rhythm. Each sequencer will have a test button on the top right which, when pressed, triggers the voice.

 

Settings

The settings section can be opened using the settings button on the home screen's bottom bar. The settings section only occupies the bottom part of the screen.

You can reset Andie to factory settings by pressing the factory reset button 5 times.

You can change Andie's look by pressing the theme button.

You can change Andie's sync mode to sync with other devices. When sync is set to "On" Andie will output a pulse every step on the left channel. When sync is set to "Pocket Operator" Andie will output a pulse every other step on the left channel. Note that in some cases the signal may need to be amplified for sync to work properly.

Sync setup example to be added.

You can record 4 bars of your current project by pressing the record button. Andie will count down 4 beats, after which the recording will start. After 4 bars have passed, or the play button has been pressed, the recording will end. NOTE: This feature is currently disabled.

The bottom bar contains the credits button and the close button (to close the settings screen).

 

Interaction

Ranged pickers

Touch and hold on any ranged picker to adjust its value. You can then move your finger to adjust that picker's value or pinch to adjust its range. Pinching outwards will expand its range and pinching inwards will reduce it.

When you're satisfied with your adjustments, you can stop holding. Pickers on the channel screen will trigger that channel's voice when you stop adjusting that picker.

 

Type pickers

Touch and hold on any type picker to adjust its value. You can then move your finger to adjust that picker's value.

When you're satisfied with your adjustments, you can stop holding. Pickers on the channel screen will trigger that channel's voice when you stop adjusting that picker.
